About

Established in 2010, we are headquartered in Bangalore with an offshore development center with 150+ HAPPY Employees (We Love What We Do).

At URS, we believe in growth: profitable and symbiotic. Growth for our clients by deciphering complex business challenges with cutting edge technology is our specialization.

URS specializes in OSS/BSS Solutions, ERP solutions, CRM, Business Intelligence, Staffing Solutions, HRMS and learning management systems.

For us, " When You Grow, We Grow " is more than a slogan: Reflection of our approach to our every engagement. Amongst the fastest growing service based company, we have our loyal clients in APAC, EMEA, America and India.

URS Technology Group (UTG)

Technology group in URS manage Technology development projects and development centres.

Telecom Consulting Group (TCG)

Consulting group help industry leader client to solve complex problem in their projects and get going.
